About Us

Hills and Associates Limited is a Malawian-owned real estate company that has been operational since 2018, with our central office currently situated at Area 47/1, Plot 47/1/121. As a matter of law, Hills and Associates Ltd is a duly registered company under the Malawi Companies Act No. 15 of 2013. The company has registered Valuation Surveyors who operate in accordance with the requirements of the Land Economy Surveyors, Valuers, Estate Agents, and Auctioneers Act (CAP 53.08). Hills and Associates was established to meet the growing need for high-quality real estate services in Malawi, which are vital for the advancement of both the real estate industry and the overall economy. With a dynamic organizational structure, extensive expertise in the Malawian real estate market, and a highly skilled team, the company is well-equipped to exceed the expectations of its clients and the real estate sector. Our main objective is to be the top choice for the public when it comes to Property Valuation, Property Management, Estate Agency and Auctioneering, and the Acquisition/Development of Prime Land.
